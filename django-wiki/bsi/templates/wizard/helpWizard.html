Virtual Assistant

The virtual assistant is a program which helps you to find articles from the BSI compendium. By asking yes/no questions, the virtual assistant can calculate which article is the most likely you are searching for.

  1. Button to return to the previous question.

  2. Restart the virtual assistant.

  3. Go to the info page.

  4. Press „Yes“ if you believe the topic is related with the article your are looking for.

  5. Press „No“ if you believe the topic is NOT related with the article you are looking for.

  6. Press „I don’t know“ if you are not sure if this topic is related.

  • Click "Articles about ..." to show example articles about the topic currently asked in the question.

  • Click on an article inside the list to open a detailed page about it.

  • Once enough questions are answered, results will be displayed.

  • Keep in mind the results are not final and if you continue to answerquestions those answers can change.
