BSI/IT Baseline Security Compendium

Note: The first edition of the IT-Grundschutz Compendium has been published. migrated the most commonly used building blocks of the 15th Supplemental Delivery of IT-Grundschutz Catalogs. The second edition expands the IT-Grundschutz Compendium with new modules and other migrated components. A unique IT-Grundschutz Compendium will be distributed to building blocks that are not part of the first edition, but will be recreated with the second edition. For target objects, in the IT-Grundschutz Compendium there are no components yet, the corresponding modules of the IT-Grundschutz Catalogs should be used. An overview of the structure of the first version of the IT-Grundschutz Compendium and the associated 80 building blocks is provided by Mindmap.
